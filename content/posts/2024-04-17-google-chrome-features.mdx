Google Chrome Features

Every Android mobile has Google. Easy to use and enhances the user experience with its best features and settings. Do these 5 settings of Google Chrome, they will make your use better, easier.

1. Language Settings

If you want to read all websites and blogs in Google Chrome in Urdu or any other language, then go to the settings "offer to send pages in other languages to Google translate" and turn it on. And below the language in which you want translation. Select

2. Sync Settings

If you want Google not to save your activity, go to Settings, go to Sync, uncheck Sync everything and from all the options below, you can turn it on or off as you like.

3. Notifications Settings

If you get a lot of notifications from other websites in Google Chrome. So, go to Chrome's settings, click on Notifications, scroll down and go to Last. Turn off all notifications that are turned on in Sites below.

4. Font Settings

If you want to capitalize or lowercase the words that appear in Chrome. So, go to Settings, go to Accessibility, Text Scaling will be written, you can adjust it as per your wish.

5. Password Manager

If you want to see which account and website passwords are saved in your Google account, then go to Settings and click on Password Manager, you will be shown the passwords of all websites and apps. You can delete, change or add a new password.

If the information is good, share it with others.
